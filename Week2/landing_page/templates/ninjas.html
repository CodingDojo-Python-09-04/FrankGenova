Ninja FAQ

  • What do Ninjas eat?

    They eat sushi, sashimi, rice, and steak tartare. Once a year they eat the heart of an ox.

  • What do Ninjas wear?

    They wear leather, weapons and masks. On halloween, the dress up as Soldiers or Star Wars characters.

  • What kind of work out does a a Ninja do?

    They work out twice a day. For cardio they run about 10 k. Then they relax with weight lifting for about 3 hours. Their favorite exercise is a pull-up.
